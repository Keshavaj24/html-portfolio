The Best Movies According to Keshava J

My top 5 Movies of all-time.

12th Fail

The real-life story of IPS Officer Manoj Kumar Sharma and IRS Officer Shraddha Joshi.

Rajakumara

It's a kannada movie, A dutiful son, a prince in his fathers' eyes, retains his purity of character despite the many challenges thrown his way.

Avengers Endgame

After the devastating events of Avengers: Infinity War (2018), the universe is in ruins. With the help of remaining allies, the Avengers assemble once more in order to reverse Thanos' actions and restore balance to the universe.

Aapthamitra

Ramesh and Ganga, a married couple, buy a palatial house, which is home to a vengeful spirit. When Ganga accidentally sets the spirit free, mysterious incidents start taking place in their household.

Jai Janaki Nayaka

It's a telegu movie, Gagan and Sweety fall in love but are forced to break up because her father disapproves of him. While defending a family from thugs one day, Gagan unknowingly saves Sweety and vows to protect her.
